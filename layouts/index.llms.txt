## Project Summary
* Fraxinus is the official documentation site for the Fraxinus Project, a fast and compact CoreXY 3D printer and digital fabrication machine project.
* The most authoritative pages are the home page, About page, model overview pages, assembly guides, and community page on this site.
* Pages are published in Japanese and English. Japanese pages are the primary source for the original project context, with English translations provided for major overview and documentation pages.
