### Printers Born from the Fraxinus Community

The Fraxinus community has produced not only custom machines directly based on Fraxinus, but also compact 3D printers shaped by knowledge and design ideas shared through the community.

#### SM-100

Compact CoreXY 3D printer designed by c-bata and powered entirely by 100W USB PD ([GitHub](https://github.com/c-bata/SM-100)). Based on experience from the Fraxinus 00 series and the Fraxinus community, SM-100 focuses on a compact footprint for small living spaces, cooling for motors and electronics, and improved fire safety.

During the build process, c-bata also shared the stainless-steel sheet-metal chassis construction.
